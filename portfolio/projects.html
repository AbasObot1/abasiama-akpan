Aquachloro Calculator

PlaceholderFrontend Dev Project

Aquachloro Calculator is a water treatment application created for the water industry. This app can calculate several parameters needed for the chlorination process, including chlorine dose, concentration, and residual chlorine; and provide the solution in just one click. It will be highly useful for drinking water, pool and spa chlorine treatment calculations. More features shall be added to the app such as those needed to resolve issues associated with shock chlorination and pH fluctuations. The app was made with HTML, CSS and JavaScript without any framework. The idea in mind is to create a simple design project with easy navigation and touch.
